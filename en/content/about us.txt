Company Salloytech Sp. z o.o. Established in 2014 and operating in the aviation industry. It deals with processing of mainly hard-working materials such as nickel and titanium superalloys in particular by means of unconventional methods and special processes. Production of parts with strict quality assurance requirements. Study of thermally sprayed coatings. We have a quality management system in accordance with AS9100: 2016 and ISO9001: 2015. Our staff has many years of experience in producing direct parts in special processes.
